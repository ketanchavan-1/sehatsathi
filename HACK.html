How It Works

Getting started with Sehat Sathi is simple. Four easy steps to better health insights.

1

Fill Health Survey

Answer a set of easy questions about your symptoms, lifestyle, and medical history — takes under 5 minutes.

2

AI Analysis

Our ML engine powered by Scikit-learn and SHAP analyzes your data to detect patterns and risk factors.

3

Get Insights

Receive a detailed health report with predicted conditions, nutrition advice, and personalized recommendations.

4

Track & Improve

Use our diet planner, voice assistant, and chatbot to follow up and continuously improve your health journey.
